Genie | The Quiz App

Genie is a quiz application that allows users to create and take quizzes on a variety of topics. The app is designed to be user-friendly and customizable, with features such as the ability to use lifelines for the answers, as well as the option to choose between multiple question types. Quizzes can be created and shared with others, and the app will also includes a leaderboard to track user progress and compare scores.
Overall, Genie is a versatile and engaging platform for both creating and taking quizzes on a wide range of topics.
